[Hook]
[Soft acoustic strumming starts]
Pull the shadows down, let the world outside just fade
I only want the space where our promises are made

And being close to you is the only thing that's right

[Verse 1]
[Gentle fingerpicked guitar and melodic bass]
I watch the way the moonlight traces over your skin
It feels like this is right where my forever begins

[Subtle ambient pads and light percussion build]

[Chorus]
[Warm drum pocket with smooth electric guitar leads]
I just want to be close to you, feeling your heartbeat fall into mine
Lost in the rhythm, crossing the line
Close to you, under the glow of the flickering light
Holding you here in the middle of the night

[Post-Chorus]
[Smooth harmonies and acoustic accents]
[Echo] (Close to you, so close to you)
Yeah, I'm exactly where I'm supposed to be, right next to you

[Verse 2]

The rest of the room just begins to clear
